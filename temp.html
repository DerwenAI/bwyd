applesauce muffins

This breakfast muffin recipe is simple to make and yields muffins which are soft, moist, high-protein, high-fiber, and packed with applesauce flavor.

duration: 29 min, 40 sec
serves: 8
  •   https://www.beatbakeeat.com/best-applesauce-muffins-recipe-ever/
  • https://www.instagram.com/p/DF1ibowRfbp/  

directions:

yields: 1 liter

Muffins use pancake batter, then bake instead of pan fry.

bowl
chilled mixing bowl
whisk
medium whisk or fork
spatula
silicone spatula

into bowl:

prep dry mix
  • add sugar: 133 g (2/3 cups)
  • add salt: 9 g (1/2 tsp)
  • add baking_soda: 50 g (1 1/2 tsp)
  • add cinnamon: 17 g (2 tsp)
  • add nutmeg: 5 g (1/2 tsp)
with whisk:
blend until well mixed, breaking any clumps
(30 sec)

 

prep wet mix
  • add applesauce: 375 g (1 1/2 cups)
  • add avocado_oil: 108 g (1/2 cups)
  • add vanilla_extract: 13 g (1 tsp)
  • add molasses: 35 g (2 tsp)
  • add egg: 2
with spatula:
fold until lightly blended
(30 sec)

 

prep chunky mix
  • add rolled_oats: 90 g (1 cups)
  • add shredded_coconut: 24 g (1/3 cups)
  • add chia_seeds: 40 g (1/4 cups)
  • add chopped_walnuts: 39 g (1/3 cups)
  • add dried_cranberries: 40 g (1/3 cups)
with spatula:
fold until lightly blended
(1 min)

 

complete the batter
  • add flour: 120 g (1 cups) — only until incorporated into the batter; do not overmix
with spatula:
mix until no parts are dry any longer
(1.5 min)

 

yields: 8

For large, moist, high-protein, high-fiber breakfast muffins.

pan
muffin pan, large size 8-hole
hand
clean hands
spoon
large spoon
fork
small fork

into pan:

grease the pan
  • add butter: 15 g (1 tsp)
with hand:
grease until cooking surfaces are all buttered
(3 min)

 

fill with batter
  • add batter: 1 l
with spoon:
fill until evenly distributed
(3 min)

 

place pan on a baking sheet, make level, set to convection bake
bake at 177 °C (350 °F)
until use fork to test doneness
(20 min)
with fork:
test doneness until fork comes out with tiny crumbs
(20 sec)
