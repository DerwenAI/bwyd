applesauce muffins

duration: 29 min, 40 sec
serves: 8

  • https://www.beatbakeeat.com/best-applesauce-muffins-recipe-ever/

  • https://www.instagram.com/p/DF1ibowRfbp/

directions:

yields: 1 l

uses:

whisk
medium whisk or fork
spatula
silicone spatula
bowl
chilled mixing bowl
into bowl
dry mix
add sugar: 133 g (2/3 cups)
add salt: 9 g (1/2 tsp)
add baking_soda: 50 g (1 1/2 tsp)
add cinnamon: 17 g (2 tsp)
add nutmeg: 5 g (1/2 tsp)

with whisk: blend until well mixed, breaking any clumps
(30 sec)

wet mix
add applesauce: 375 g (1 1/2 cups)
add avocado_oil: 108 g (1/2 cups)
add vanilla_extract: 13 g (1 tsp)
add molasses: 35 g (2 tsp)
add egg: 2

with spatula: fold until lightly blended
(30 sec)

chunky mix
add rolled_oats: 90 g (1 cups)
add shredded_coconut: 24 g (1/3 cups)
add chia_seeds: 40 g (1/4 cups)
add chopped_walnuts: 39 g (1/3 cups)
add dried_cranberries: 40 g (1/3 cups)

with spatula: fold until lightly blended
(1 min)

add flour: 120 g (1 cups) — only until incorporated into the batter; do not overmix

with spatula: mix until no parts are dry any longer
(1.5 min)

for large, moist, breakfast muffins

yields: 8

uses:

hand
clean hands
spoon
large spoon
fork
small fork
pan
muffin pan, large size 8-hole
into pan
add butter: 15 g (1 tsp)

with hand: grease until cooking surfaces are all buttered
(3 min)

add batter: 1 l

with spoon: fill until evenly distributed
(3 min)

place pan on a baking sheet, make level, set to convection bake
bake at 177 °C (350 °F) for 20 min
until use fork to test doneness

with fork: test doneness until comes out with tiny crumbs
(10 sec)
